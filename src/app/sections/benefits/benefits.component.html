Why to think about our food?
Eating healthy personalized meals gives you much more advantages than you could even think about it. Lets consider this examples.
Health
When our body weight is at the right level and we provide the body with the right level of macro nutrients, we reduce the risk of many diseases.
Economic
On average, every year we waste food with the value of a new phone. It is worth using this money in a different way.
Ecologic
We are wasting the Earth's resources, which we have less and less, and thus exacerbating the problems associated with climate change.
